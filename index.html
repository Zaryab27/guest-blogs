Process

From draft to
published — simply.

Our editorial process is designed to be fast, fair, and frictionless. We review every submission personally and respond within 3 business days.

01

Submit Your Pitch

Fill out our submission form with your topic, angle, and a brief outline. No full draft required at this stage.

02

Editorial Review

Our editors review your pitch for fit, originality, and value to our readers. Response within 3 business days.

03

Refine & Polish

If approved, collaborate with our team to perfect your piece — structure, clarity, and style all considered.

04

Go Live

Your article is published and promoted across our channels. You receive a full author profile and byline.
