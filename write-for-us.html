FAQ

Common questions.

Is guest posting free?

Yes! Submitting and publishing a guest post on QuickCraft Blog is completely free. We do not charge any fees.

How long before I get a response?

We review all submissions within 3 business days and respond to every email, whether accepted or not.

Can I include links in my article?

You may include one do-follow backlink to your own website. Internal links to relevant content on our blog are also welcome.

Do you accept previously published content?

No. We only accept 100% original content that has not been published on any other website, blog, or platform.

Can I submit more than one article?

Absolutely! Once your first article is published, you are welcome to submit more. Regular contributors get priority review.

Will you edit my article?

We may make minor edits for clarity, formatting, or grammar. Major changes will always be discussed with you first.
